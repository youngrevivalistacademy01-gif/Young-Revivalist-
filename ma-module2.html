Dominion Over the Blackspot

Victory in the Inner Man

Reclaiming the Soul's Territory

The 'Blackspot' represents those hidden areas of the soul—unresolved trauma, secret sins, or mental strongholds—that the enemy uses as a legal landing strip. In this session, we identify these internal compromise points and apply the Word of God to evict every foreign influence. We move from being 'haunted' by the past to having full dominion in the present.

Identifying the Landing Strip

Learning how to perform a spiritual audit to find where the enemy has found a 'place' (topos) in your soul.

Enforcing the Eviction

Using the authority of Christ to break soul ties, dismantle strongholds, and seal the 'Blackspot' with the Blood.
