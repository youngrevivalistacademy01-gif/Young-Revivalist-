About the Class

This is a 6-week basic growth course. It is automatic yet demands your full commitment. The timetable will dictate reviews of your specific topics.

Completing this journey leads to a physical retreat, graduation, certification, and an ordination service.

  • Review & assignment session after each class [8pm - 10pm].
  • 70% attendance & complete reviews are mandatory for every student.
  • Tuesdays & Thursdays are dedicated to morning Pauline prayers on Google Meet.
  • Missing 3 consecutive prayer sessions will attract an official query.
  • Absence during review sessions will attract an administrative query.
  • Financial Commitment: The graduation & retreat convention is billed at ₦20,000 (₦15,000 for retreat & graduation ceremony; ₦5,000 for materials & administrative fees).

Disqualifying Behaviors

The following actions will lead to immediate review or direct removal from the cohort:

Repeated absence or consistent lateness to sessions.
Failing to submit assignments or disrespecting facilitators.
Cheating, AI usage, or academic plagiarism.
Dishonoring the consecrated Spirit of the training.
