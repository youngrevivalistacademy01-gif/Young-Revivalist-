About the Class

This is a 16-week basic growth course. It is automatic yet demands your full commitment. The timetable will dictate reviews of your specific topics.

Completing this journey leads to a physical retreat, graduation, certification, and an ordination service.

  • There would be a review & assignment after each classes [8pm-10pm]
  • 70% Of attendance & review is compulsory for each student
  • Tuesdays & Thursdays would be for morning pauline prayers on google meet
  • Missing 3 consecutive prayer sessions would lead to a query
  • Absence during the review sessions would lead to a query from the administrator

    • Disqualifying Behaviors

    The following will lead to immediate review or removal:

    Repeated absence or consistent lateness.
    Failing assignments or disrespecting facilitators.
    Cheating, AI usage, or plagiarism.
    Dishonoring the Spirit of the training.
