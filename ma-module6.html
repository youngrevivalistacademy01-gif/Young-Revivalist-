Session 6.0

Practical Soul Winning

He Who Wins Souls is Wise — Prov 11:30

Part 1: Connecting with People

Engagement begins with genuine interest. Focus on the person’s secular life—their origin, family, and hobbies. Use open-ended questions (Why, How, What) and offer sincere compliments to open the heart before transitioning to diagnostic spiritual questions.

Active Listening

Give the gift of being heard. Maintain eye contact, echo keywords, and be sensitive to emotional cues during the conversation.

Diagnostic Questions

Gently shift the focus: "Do you know why God created you?" or "Have you thought about where you will spend eternity?"

Part 2: The Gospel Message

GRACE: Heaven is a free gift (Rom 6:23). We cannot earn it.
MAN: All have sinned and fall short (Rom 3:23). God's standard is perfection.
GOD: He is Loving (1 John 4:8) but also Just (Exo 34:7). Sin must be judged.
JESUS: The Solution. He died and rose to pay our penalty (1 Pet 2:24).
COMMITMENT: Receive Jesus as Lord and Savior (Rom 10:9-10).

Part 3: Follow-Up & Discipleship

Evangelism is incomplete without a reproducing disciple. Ensure the new believer has assurance of salvation (John 5:13) and follows the Five Means of Growth:

  • Bible (Read John)
  • Prayer (CATIP Model)
  • Worship (Church)
  • Fellowship (Community)
  • Witness (Testimony)
