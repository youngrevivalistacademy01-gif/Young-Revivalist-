MODULE OVERVIEW

Human history began in a state of profound crisis. The fall introduction brought sin and total spiritual separation between man and the Creator. Left to our own devices, humanity faced an insurmountable deficit—completely incapable of bridging the gulf.

But the turning point arrived at Calvary. Through the Cross and Redemption, Jesus offered a definitive, flawless solution. His substitutionary sacrifice fully absorbed the penalty of sin, transforming a verdict of condemnation into an eternal legacy of freedom.

This absolute turnaround is accessed entirely by Grace through Faith. It is not earned or manufactured by performance; it is a gift that sparks an immediate New Birth—instantly shifting your status into full, unshakeable Assurance of Salvation. This is the true cornerstone of understanding the Gospel.
