The Tripartite Design

To walk in maturity, one must understand the architecture of their being. Man is a spirit, he has a soul, and he lives in a body. In this module, we dissect the interaction between these three realms. We explore how the 'Psycho' (Soul/Mind) can either hinder or facilitate the flow of the Spirit, and how to bring all three into divine alignment.
