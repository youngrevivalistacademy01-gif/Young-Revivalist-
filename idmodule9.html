The Faith Banquet

Feasting on the Promises of God

A Table Prepared

The Faith Banquet is an invitation to transition from spiritual hunger to divine abundance. It is not enough to know the promises exist; we must learn how to take our seat at the table and partake of the bread of life. In this session, we explore how faith acts as the hand that receives what grace has already provided.

The Cup of Covenant
