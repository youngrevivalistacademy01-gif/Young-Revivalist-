MODULE OVERVIEW

Your legal position determines your earthly experience. Discovering exactly who I am in Christ uncovers the reality of sonship and inheritance. You are no longer an outsider trying to earn access; you are an heir operating directly from a secure Kingdom identity.

This legal position carries active leverage. As a believer, you have been trusted with real spiritual authority to enforce the victory of the cross here on earth. This is the activation of the original dominion mandate over circumstances, environments, and spiritual opposition.

True authority manifests practically. Embracing your configuration in Christ gives you the structural leverage for walking in victory over sin. Defeat is broken, and a clean pattern of life becomes your daily reality.
