Advanced Deliverance Class

Enforcing the Victory of the Cross

The Foundational Truth

Deliverance must be anchored in the Finished Work of Christ. Satan and demons do not own believers. While they may influence, harass, or oppress, they cannot possess those indwelt by the Holy Spirit (1 Cor 6:19). Freedom is maintained through the renewal of the mind and active discipleship.

Biblically Balanced Influence

1. Oppression

External harassment affecting emotions or health. Solution: Resist the devil and walk in the Spirit.

2. Suppression

Satanic resistance blocking progress or causing stagnation. Solution: Persistence in prayer and divine wisdom.

3. Obsession

Mind occupied by destructive thoughts/lies. Solution: Mind renewal and emotional healing.

4. Possession

Complete control of the will (Unbelievers only). Solution: Direct deliverance and Gospel proclamation.

Understanding Curses

A curse is a released destruction operating through disobedience or words. While Christ redeemed us positionally (Gal 3:13), freedom must be enforced experientially. Curses lose their power where truth is known and authority is active.

Note: Deliverance without mental renewal and discipleship is ineffective.
