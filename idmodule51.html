Session 5.1

Intimacy: The Secret Place

Deepening the Interior Life

Cultivating the Garden

In Part Two, we transition from the revelation of fatherhood to the discipline of devotion. Intimacy is a garden that must be tended. We explore how to create a consistent 'Secret Place'—a spiritual environment where your soul is restored and your spirit is recalibrated by His presence, far beyond the noise of the world.

The Art of Abiding

Moving from 'visiting' God’s presence to 'dwelling' there. Understanding the mechanics of staying connected throughout a busy day.

The Quiet of the Soul

Learning how to silence internal noise (anxiety, to-do lists, and performance) to hear the whispers of the Father.
