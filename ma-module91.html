Practical Module 9.1

Ministering Deliverance

Practical Steps and Ministerial Ethics

The 5-Step Process

Step 1: Repentance — Confession and turning away (Acts 3:19).
Step 2: Forgiveness — Breaking bitterness and resentment (Matt 18:34).
Step 3: Renunciation — Verbally breaking wrong agreements and lies.
Step 4: Exercising Authority — Command firmly, do not beg or shout (Luke 10:19).
Step 5: Follow the Spirit — Never force manifestations; allow for persistence.

"In the name of Jesus Christ, I command every unclean spirit to leave now."

Infilling & Renewal

Freedom is maintained through the infilling of the Spirit (Eph 5:18) and the renewal of the mind (Rom 12:2).

Discipleship

Encourage fellowship, mentorship, and a daily spiritual structure to build maturity.

Ministerial Ethics

  • Do NOT create fear.
  • Do NOT shame or expose.
  • Do NOT replace discipleship.
  • ALWAYS protect dignity.
  • ALWAYS emphasize Christ.
  • ALWAYS combine with teaching.
Deliverance is an entry point, not the end.
